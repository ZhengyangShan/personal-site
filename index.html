I am a PhD student in the Faculty of Computing and Data Sciences at Boston University. I study fairness, interpretability, and evaluation of large language models, focusing on how demographic attributes are represented and their potential misuse.

My long-term vision is to build robust, bias-aware language technologies that remain transparent and accountable under distribution shift.
